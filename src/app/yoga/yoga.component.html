Shoulder Exercises

What is shoulders workout?

Shoulder exercises are vital for developing strong, functional, and aesthetically pleasing deltoid muscles. These exercises target the anterior (front), medial (side), and posterior (rear) heads of the deltoids, contributing to overall upper body strength and stability. Exercises range from presses that engage multiple shoulder heads to raises that isolate specific areas. A balanced shoulder routine incorporates variations to ensure comprehensive development of all deltoid regions.
