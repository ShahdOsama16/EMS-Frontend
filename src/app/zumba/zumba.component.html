What is Back Exercises?

Back exercises are crucial for building a strong, wide, and thick back, contributing significantly to overall upper body strength, posture, and injury prevention. These exercises target a complex group of muscles including the latissimus dorsi, rhomboids, trapezius, and erector spinae. Movements range from pulling exercises like rows and pull-ups that build width and thickness, to exercises that focus on the upper and lower back for stability and posture. A comprehensive back workout incorporates a variety of these movements to ensure balanced development.
