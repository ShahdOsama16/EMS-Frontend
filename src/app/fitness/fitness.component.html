Bi & Tri Exercises

What is Bi & Tri?

Biceps and triceps exercises are essential for well-rounded arm development, focusing on the front and back of the upper arms, respectively. Biceps exercises, like curls, primarily target the muscles responsible for elbow flexion and forearm supination. Triceps exercises, such as extensions and presses, work the muscles that extend the elbow joint. A balanced routine incorporating various exercises for both muscle groups ensures comprehensive arm strength and definition.
