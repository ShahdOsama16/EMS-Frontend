What is the EMS?

EMS is a professional electrostimulation system that utilizes whole-body electrical muscle stimulation (WB-EMS) to enhance fitness results. By Putting Patches on the Muscles, it simultaneously activates over 300 muscles, offering time-efficient 20-minute workouts. This technology aims to improve sports performance, muscle toning, weight loss, rehabilitation, and pain reduction, featuring advanced software for precise training parameter control and compatibility with body composition analysis. EMS provides a versatile and efficient solution for individuals seeking comprehensive muscle activation and diverse fitness benefits.
