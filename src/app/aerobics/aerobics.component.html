What is Legs Exercises?

Leg exercises are foundational for building lower body strength, power, and muscle mass. They target major muscle groups including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves, contributing to overall athleticism and stability. Exercises range from compound movements like squats and deadlifts that work multiple leg muscles simultaneously to isolation exercises such as leg extensions and hamstring curls. A comprehensive leg routine incorporates a variety of exercises to ensure balanced development of all lower body muscle groups.
